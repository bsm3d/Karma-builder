BSM KARMA BUILDER - AI TRANSPARENCY STATEMENT
=============================================

Author: Benoit Saint-Moulin
Year: 2026
Web: https://www.bsm3d.com

BSM Karma Builder was developed as a Houdini procedural lighting and render setup tool from practical VFX production experience. This document explains clearly where AI tools were used
and where they were not.


WHAT WAS HUMAN-LED
------------------

- Product direction and feature priorities
- Application architecture and module design
- UI structure, dialogs, editor behavior, and workflow choices
- Core implementation decisions and integration strategy
- Final review and validation before acceptance


HOW AI WAS USED
---------------

AI was used as an assistant for support tasks such as:

- style harmonization and naming consistency
- documentation cleanup and phrasing improvements
- bug-tracing support and cross-checking edge cases

Any AI-suggested output was reviewed and edited before inclusion.


WHAT AI DID NOT DO
------------------

- AI did not independently define BSM Karma Builder's architecture.
- AI did not autonomously build or ship the product.
- AI did not make release decisions.
- AI did not replace human testing and validation.


SUMMARY
-------

BSM Karma Builder is a human-authored software project. AI has been used as a productivity
assistant, not as an autonomous author.
